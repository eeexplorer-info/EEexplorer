Purple collar refers to a mix between white collar and blue collar. Jobs in this category can combine manual labour and administrative duties. These professionals have usually pursued higher education in a specific field that requires the regular use of heavy machinery. An example of these employees is professionals who specialise in technology, like engineers and technicians.
