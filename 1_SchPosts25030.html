KJ		 Celebrating our ARC Discovery Project 2026 awardees!

We're proud of our researchers driving innovation across space, energy, communications, and advanced manufacturing:

- Prof Andrew Dempster - Developing PNT techniques for Australia's lunar mission.

- A/Prof Branislav Hredzak & Prof John Fletcher - Optimising electrified transport fleet operations.

- A/Prof Derrick Wing Kwan Ng & Prof Jinhong Yuan - Advancing mixed near- and far-field communication for next-gen wireless.

- Prof Gang-Ding Peng - Creating 3D manufacturing for specialty silica optical fibres.

- A/Prof Guo Chen - Designing cooperative architectures for virtual power plants.

- A/Prof Vidhyasaharan Sethu - Contributing to a multiscale design approach for photocatalytic methane oxidation.

Congratulations to all Chief Investigators and collaborators!
