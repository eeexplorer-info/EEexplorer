Quotes Compiled - Tao Te Ching


== Final Conclusion ==

"True words are not beautiful;
Beautiful words are not true.

A good man does not argue;
He who argues is not a good man.

A wise man with no extensive knowledge is not a wise man.

The sage does not accumulate for himself.
The more he uses for others, the more he has himself.
The more he gives to others, the more he posses of his own.

The way of Heaven is to benefit others and not to injure.

The way of the sage is to act but not compete."
/ Tao Te Ching /
