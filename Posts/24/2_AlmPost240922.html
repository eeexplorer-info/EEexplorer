0INTRODUCTION
Since we've had the Spring Equinox today, life should rapidly gain momentum toward Christmas and its Summer Solstice. As you may know, it's the zero to "pi on two" part of the yearly sinewave.

1ELSOC BBQ
My associated graphic is a summary of the how it flowed last Tuesday, 17th September.

From the first floor, you could tell the attendees had an association with ElecEng. If I say, the line-up resembled an under-damped response to a step-input signal, you'll understand.

The queue maintained it's shape and stepped about every 15-20 seconds for at-least an hour from 12:45 pm.

The Elsoc crew passed the tongs & restocked the grill seamlessly like athletes passing a baton in an Olympic race. Nothing was dropped. It was a good time to interact with the undergraduates & staff that briefly appeared. Even the Head of School's face reflected the charged activity.
