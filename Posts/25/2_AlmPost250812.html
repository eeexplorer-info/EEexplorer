4JOB RESPONSIBILITIES

From advertisement:

- Lead the development and continuous improvement of asset strategies, policies and standards informed by FMECA and lifecycle analysis, ensuring our network is safe, reliable and cost-effective throughout its lifespan.

- Oversee preventative and condition-based maintenance practices, working closely with Field Operations and the EPMO to ensure proactive risk management and consistent asset performance.

- Manage vegetation strategies in partnership with Major Contracts and Procurement teams, supporting safe and efficient network operations.

- Collaborate with internal experts to design and maintain robust asset risk and intervention value models that guide asset strategies, investment decisions and climate resilience planning.

- Drive long-term asset planning and forecasting, developing intervention strategies and maintenance programs that balance performance, safety and sustainability.

- Lead condition monitoring and use data insights to identify performance issues and recommend responsive improvements beyond standard practices.

- Support team development, mentoring and inclusive leadership while contributing to the delivery of Endeavour Energy's Network Business and Corporate Strategies.

- Offer trusted technical advice across teams to support network expansion, incident recovery and problem-solving.

- Represent Endeavour Energy on industry panels, fostering collaboration and alignment on technical standards and best practices.
