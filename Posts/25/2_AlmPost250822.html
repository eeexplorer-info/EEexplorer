4OLD HUMOUR - ELECTRICIAN RATES

Here's another quickly scribbled gem from my diary.

It was from a chap that worked with us in the early 1990's when I was circulating between the office and the field with our magnetic door access security products. False alarms were headaches to track down.


$100 / hr - Minimum
$150 / hr - If you watch

$175 / hr - If you help
$200 / hr - If you worked on it first

$250 / hr - If you tell me how to do my job

-- Tonight's addition:
$300 / hr - If you mention "I saw it on the internet"


The prices have been adjusted to reflect current rates.

Could this be helpful to our micro-managed friends to know where they are in the ladder?
