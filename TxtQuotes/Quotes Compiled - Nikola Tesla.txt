Quotes Compiled - Nikola Tesla



"When wireless is perfectly applied the whole earth will be converted into a huge brain, which in fact it is, all things being particles of a real and rhythmic whole.

We shall be able to communicate with one another instantly, irrespective of distance. Not only this, but through television and telephony we shall see and hear one another as perfectly as though we were face to face, despite intervening distances of thousands of miles; and the instruments through which we shall be able to do his will be amazingly simple compared with our present telephone.

A man will be able to carry one in his vest pocket."
~~ Nikola Tesla ( May 26, 1919 New Yorker Hotel ) ~~


"You look at these characteristics the way a sailor watches the wind."
~~ Nikola Tesla


"Contrary to general belief, work comes easier for older people if they are in good health, because they have learned through years of practice how to arrive at a given place by the shortest path."
~~ Nikola Tesla


"But instinct is something which transcends knowledge. We have, undoubtedly, certain finer fibers that enable us to perceive truths when logical deduction, or any other willful effort of the brain, is futile."
~~ Nikola Tesla in "My Inventions"


"If you want to find the secrets of the universe, think in terms of energy, frequency and vibration."
~~ Nikola Tesla


"My brain is only a receiver, in the Universe there is a core from which we obtain knowledge, strength and inspiration."
~~ Nikola Tesla
