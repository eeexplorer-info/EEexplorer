DEFLATING ACTIONS


CLUB or MEETING

1DON'T COME TO THE MEETINGS.
2BUT IF YOU DO COME -- COME LATE.

3NEVER ACCEPT AN OFFICE, AS IT IS EASIER TO CRITICISE THAN TO DO THINGS.
4IF ASKED BY THE CHAIRMAN TO GIVE YOUR OPINION ABOUT SOME IMPORTANT MATTER, TELL HIM YOU HAVE NOTHING TO SAY.

5AFTER THE MEETING, TELL EVERYONE HOW THINGS OUGHT TO BE DONE.
6HOLD BACK YOUR DUES AS LONG AS POSSBILE, OR BETTER STILL DON'T PAY AT ALL.

7IF YOU AGREE WITH EVERYTHING AT THE MEETING, MAKE SURE TO DISAGREE WITH IT OUTSIDE.
8WHEN EVERTHING ELSE FAILS, ABUSE THE OFFICE-BEARERS.

9DON'T BOTHER ABOUT GETTING NEW MEMBERS - LET THE SECRETARY DO THAT.
10TALK CO-OPERATION FOR THE OTHER FELLOW WITH YOU BUT NEVER CO-OPERATE WITH HIM.

11WHEN ASKED FOR INFORMATION -- DON'T GIVE IT.
12DON'T TELL THE CLUB HOW IT CAN HELP YOU, BUT IF IT DOESN'T HELP YOUU, TELL EVERYBODY IT IS USELESS.

13CUSS THE CLUB FOR THE INCOMPLETENESS OF ITS INFORMATION.
14DO NOTHING MORE THAN NECESSARY BUT WHEN OTHER MEMBERS ROLL UP THEIR SLEEVES AND WILLINGLY, UNSELFISHLY USE THEIR ABILITY TO HELP MATTERS ALONG, COMPLAIN THAT THE CLUB IS RUN BY A CLIQUE.


(*)Presented in all humility in the hope that members will find them humorous, entertaining and perhaps, thought provoking. -- Shared by a friend in the 1980's.
