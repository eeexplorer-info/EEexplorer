3FIELDED QUESTIONS

At our EE&T Alumni table, James Bott from AEMO, joined us this year. Some questions were:

+ How long has it been since you graduated? Where do you work now and what do they do?

+ What opportunities are there for a mechatronics graduate in the industries where electrical engineers work?

+ What benefits are there in getting to know other students also studying electrical engineering, for example by participating in events organised by Elsoc, etc.

+ Is it better to start in consulting or get other work experience before going into consulting?

+ Would you review my resume and make improvements?

+ Did you graduate from this Uni?

+ Do you have any first project tips for interviews?

+ When would job prospects be better in the Power Industry - before or after a PhD?

+ What work path did you take after you graduated?
