Quotes Compiled - Napoleon Hill


"The moment anyone accepts favors, he is in debt to the one
granting them. Sooner or later the debt must be paid."
/ Napoleon Hill /


"Good credit is a great asset provided it is not overused."
~~ Napoleon Hill


"A truly big man never tries to impress others with his bigness and never tries to keep up with the Joneses."
~~ Napoleon Hill


"Self-praise is a credit only when it consists of deeds helpful to others and not of mere words."
-- Napoleon Hill --


"No man ever becomes so successful that he doesn’t appreciate a kindly word of commendation for work well done."
/ Napoleon Hill /


"You can always tell a man who thinks he is smarter than others, but you can’t tell him much."
~~ Napoleon Hill ~~


"The man who listens more than he talks generally knows more than the man who talks more than he listens."
-- Napoleon Hill


"Discipline comes through self control. This means that you must control all negative qualities. Before you can control conditions, you must first control yourself. Self-mastery is the hardest job you will ever tackle. If you do not conquer self, you will be conquered by self."
/ Napoleon Hill /


"A temper is a good thing to have provided one does not try to give it to someone else."
~~ Napoleon Hill ~~


"If you must speak ill of another, do not speak it—write it in the sand near the water’s edge."
~~ Napoleon Hill ~~
