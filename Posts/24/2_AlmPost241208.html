3BENCH MARKED AREAS

Strengths: Culture, Supportive Environment, People working together, Laboratories are very good.

To improve: more Machine Learning (AI) needed and more on Embedded Systems.

The Imperial College of London inspectors stayed the whole week in ElecEng while the students were in term for the bench marking accreditation.

Thank you Prof. Jinhong Yuan for sharing privileged information.
