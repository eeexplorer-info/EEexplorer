2SBS TV BRIEF

"Your Brain, On Tech: Whether it's social media, artificial intelligence chatbots or banking, news and maps all on a device in your hip pocket, technology seems non-negotiable.

But, do the costs outweigh the conveniences? Host Kumi Taguchi speaks with addicts, abstainers, and those in between to explore whether we're too reliant on our digital devices."

5CONNECT NIGHT

There was a presentation back in 2015 by Dr. Geoff Mackellar to the Alumni. It was titled, "Wearables for the Brain".

What I only have in the email is:

"This year we are excited to host Dr. Geoff Mackellar, CTO of Emotiv Inc. who will be presenting a lecture titled, "Emotiv EPOC and Insight - Wearables for the Brain."

With the interest around wearable devices growing each day, it is sure to be an insightful address. More details on Dr. Geoff Mackellar's work can be found in the attached event flyer.

The event is also your last chance to see the School of Electrical Engineering & Telecommunications building before its upcoming $104 million refurbishment. There will be an opportunity to reminisce on your university days with a tour to the labs and around the building."

If I find more information on it apart from it being on 19th October, I'll share it in a comment.
