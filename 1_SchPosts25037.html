Clay Chu, Prof Rukmi Dutta, and John Fletcher recently showcased their progress in scaling and commercialising the School's IP in high-speed electromagnetic machine design to Shanghai's National Innovation Center par Excellence (NICE) and Wuxi Industrial Innovation Research Institute (WIIRI) as part of the UNSW-NICE collaboration.

Excited to build on this momentum and explore new opportunities for global collaboration!
