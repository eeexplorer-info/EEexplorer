1ELSOC AGM
It was non-stop for 2.5 hours from 8 pm. Someone forgot to order the Pizza!

On entering the room, voters picked a number out of a hat. When it was time to vote, they scanned the displayed barcode and entered their associated 9 hex digit number - with the dashes of course. The online voters had their own identifier.

There was even a 3:30 am video call to Liam Mitchell in Canada over the local Discord channel for an Industry Exec position presentation. Jerry Yan had more votes for it. Liam will have the Socials Exec portfolio in 2025 when he's back from his Leadership course. Obviously, he's not in the Exec photo from the night.

4DAYLIGHT SAVING

For the ones who are working overseas, next weekend is the start of it for us. It may help you time your Conference or Family calls. Clocks go forward an hour here on Sunday 2 am. It'll revert back on 06 April.

The following TimeAndDate website has been helpful over the years and being in different countries -- Click(TimeAndDate). If you have a favourite, please share in the comments.

Full: https://www.timeanddate.com/time/dst/daylight-savings-time.html
