7DUSTY SUGGESTION

No time or missing the randomly fed posts on the social pages? The following may be obvious to you.

Tag the home-page as a bookmark in your browser or make a home screen icon and launch them when you have a window of time.

It developed from my batch file menus pointing to remote places on the ElecEng Unix system in the early 1980's. The system dictionary, w2006, had a tricky path to recall & type - (/usr/lib/dict/ ?)
