4WELCOME SCREEN

If you have graduated from ElecEng (UNSW) with an 'Under', 'Post', 'Master', 'Doctorate', 'Professor', etc on your LI page then you're eligible.

It may help synchronise your read/write edges or zero-crossover points (analogue world).


Here are some interpreted guidelines from the LI group. These were written before I joined it in mid 2019.

- share knowledge, experience, and memories,
- keep in touch with the School of EE&T,

- develop networks across different industries,
- reconnect alumni through activities,

- 'give back' to the School in any way.


There were many requests in the year to join. A lot were declined. Some had blank profiles. Some had overseas qualifications.
