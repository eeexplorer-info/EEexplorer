4SCHOLARSHIP OUTLINE

Started by Luke Welfare in 2023 from the 1980's ElecEng group as a perpetual scholarship.

To have funds in-place for undergraduate students who come from a low social economic background.

"It's to help the student complete their degree."

"The world needs more Electrical Engineers as things are getting smarter."

Prof. Julien Epps, at the time, contributed some School funds toward it.
