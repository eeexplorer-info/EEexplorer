1THESIS POSTER COMPETITION

It was held in ElecEng from 4:30 - 6:30 pm on the First Floor instead of the usual 6:30 - 8:30 pm. Last Wednesday, 20th November. My first visit to this yearly event.

There were 17 selected posters with their undergraduates to explain and answer questions. The mingling crowd had judges with QR codes to score each poster.
