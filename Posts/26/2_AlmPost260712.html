1INTRODUCTION1

Thank you to my direct contacts for considering the offer to attend the 10th Elsoc Alumni night at The Roundhouse. This also extends to the sub-groups from them.

There were many other functions on that first Friday evening in July this year. The 24 hour day now has more work, family and personal demands than it did for the 2016 gathering.

Thank you to the brave undergraduates who attended, asked or listened on the night with their mid-term exams to start in two-days.
