KJ		 EE&T Alumni Community: 2025 Wrap-Up & Looking Ahead to 2026

As we welcome the new year, we reflect on a vibrant 2025 filled with reconnections, community growth, and strong engagement across our EE&T alumni networks.

Highlights included milestone events such as the 70th and 40th EE Reunions, ELSoC Industry Night, the 9th ELSoC - Alumni Night, and the launch of the EE&T Community LinkedIn page. Our community also continues to grow, welcoming new members in both 2025 and early 2026, strengthening our alumni network across generations and geographies.

Thank you to Robert Smith and everyone who contributed, organised events, shared updates, and stayed connected throughout the year. We look forward to another year of collaboration, celebration, and community in 2026.
